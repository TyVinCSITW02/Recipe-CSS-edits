Bacon-Wrapped Stuffed Jalapeños

I want to share a recipe made by Sally.This recipe is great for any season and is also quick and easy to make.It is perfect as an appetizer or paired with foods like ribs and tacos.

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Jalepenos (halved)
  • Spices of your Choice(garlic, paprika, salt)
  • Bacon

Preparation

  1. Cut the jalepenos in half lengthwise and remove the seeds
  2. In a seperate bowl, combine the cream cheese, cheddar cheese, spices until it is a smooth, even mixture
  3. Fill each pepper half with the cheese filling
  4. Wrap a piece of bacon around each pepper half
  5. Bake the peppers until the bacon is crispy on the outside

Why this is my favorite recipe

Stuffed jalapeños are great for ay occasion. They are a great appetizer to serve at house parties, during football games, or at potlucks. This is a recipe that can be used during any season; throw them on the grill in the summer or in the oven in the fall.

Stuffed jalapeños are quick and easy to make too. They would make a great activity to do with your family and friends just like with baking cookies or making finger foods.
